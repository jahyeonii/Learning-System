🏛️ The Magic Library

Read the story then answer the questions below!

⏱ 8 min📖 Story + Quiz⚡ 50 XP

The Story

In the middle of the town of Willowbrook stood a very unusual building. It had tall stone walls covered in ivy, stained-glass windows that glowed with every color of the rainbow, and a sign above the door that read: THE MAGIC LIBRARY.

A girl named Mia had walked past it every day on her way to school, but she had never dared to go inside — until one rainy Tuesday.

She pushed open the heavy wooden door and stepped in. The smell of old books and warm cinnamon filled the air. Thousands of books lined the shelves, all the way up to the ceiling.

A kind librarian with silver hair and round glasses looked up. "Welcome, Mia. We've been expecting you."

Mia blinked. "How do you know my name?"

The librarian smiled and pointed to a glowing blue book on a nearby shelf. "That one has been waiting for you."

Mia picked up the book. The cover read: The Adventures of Mia — Chapter One. She gasped. Her name was on the cover!

She opened the first page and began to read. The words described exactly where she was standing, right at that moment, in the Magic Library.

"Every reader has a story," the librarian said softly. "This library helps you find yours."

Mia sat down in a cozy armchair and read until the rain stopped. When she left, she tucked the book safely under her arm — and promised herself she would be back tomorrow.
