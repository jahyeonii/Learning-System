What are Silent Letters?

Some letters are written in a word but not said out loud. These are called silent letters!

Silent K
K is silent before N
🔪 Knife🦵 Knee
Silent W
W is silent before R
✍️ Write🔧 Wrench
Silent E
E at end changes vowel
🎂 cake🦴 bone
Silent B
B is silent after M
💣 bomb🌿 climb
