Read This First! 📖

Maya loved rainy days. While other kids groaned when clouds gathered in the sky, Maya ran to get her yellow boots and her favourite green umbrella.

One afternoon, a huge storm rolled in over her town. Thunder rumbled and lightning flashed, but Maya wasn't scared. She sat by her bedroom window, watching the rain race down the glass in tiny rivers.

Her cat, Biscuit, curled up in her lap and purred softly. Maya stroked his orange fur and sipped a mug of hot chocolate.

Suddenly, she noticed something in the puddle on the street below. A tiny paper boat was sailing along the gutter — someone had made it and set it free!

Maya smiled. She grabbed a piece of paper, folded it carefully into a boat shape, and wrote her name on the side: S.S. Maya. Then she ran outside, boots splashing through every puddle, and set her boat sailing down the street.

She watched it float until it disappeared around the corner, off on its own little adventure.
