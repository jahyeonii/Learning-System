🐠 Captain Coral's Quest

Read the story then answer the questions below!

⏱ 10 min📖 Story + Quiz⚡ 50 XP

The Story

Deep beneath the glittering ocean waves, in a reef made of pink coral and golden sand, lived a small but very brave fish named Captain Coral. She had shimmering blue and orange stripes and a tiny captain's hat she had found tangled in some seaweed.

One morning, Captain Coral heard the older fish whispering about the Pearl of the Deep — a legendary pearl said to glow with a light so bright it could guide lost sailors home from any storm.

"I'm going to find it," Captain Coral announced.

The older fish shook their heads. "The pearl is guarded by the Giant Clam at the bottom of the Midnight Trench," said an old seahorse. "No fish has ever returned."

But Captain Coral was not afraid. She packed a small bag of seaweed snacks and swam down, deeper and deeper, past glowing jellyfish, sleeping sharks, and forests of swaying kelp.

At the bottom of the trench, she found the Giant Clam. It was enormous — the size of a house — and its shell was tightly shut.

Captain Coral did not try to force it open. Instead, she sang the clam a gentle song her mother had taught her. Slowly, slowly, the clam opened its shell, revealing the Pearl of the Deep, glowing soft and warm like a small sun.

"You may take it," the Giant Clam rumbled. "You are the first to ask with kindness instead of force."

Captain Coral carried the pearl back to the reef. That night, its warm glow lit up the whole ocean floor, and every fish, crab, and sea turtle gathered around to see it.

And from that day on, no sailor was ever lost at sea again.
