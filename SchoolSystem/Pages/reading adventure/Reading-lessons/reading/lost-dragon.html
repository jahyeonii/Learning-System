🐉 The Lost Dragon

Read the story then answer the questions below!

⏱ 5 min 📖 Story + Quiz ⚡ 50 XP

The Story

Once upon a time, deep in the Emerald Mountains, there lived a small dragon named Ember. Ember had bright orange scales and tiny golden wings that sparkled in the sunlight.

One sunny morning, Ember decided to explore the forest beyond the mountains. He flew over tall trees, past a shimmering waterfall, and through a field of purple flowers.

But when Ember turned around to fly home — he didn't know which way to go! The mountains all looked the same from above.

"Oh no," said Ember. "I'm lost!"

Just then, a friendly owl named Ollie landed on a branch nearby. "Are you okay, little dragon?" Ollie asked.

"I can't find my way home," Ember said sadly.

Ollie smiled. "I know every path in this forest. Follow me!"

Together they flew over the waterfall, past the purple flowers, and back through the tall trees. Soon, Ember could see the Emerald Mountains glowing in the afternoon sun.

"Home!" Ember cheered, blowing a happy puff of fire into the sky.

From that day on, Ember always brought Ollie a gift of golden berries whenever he visited — and the two became the very best of friends.
