Learn the Vowels

Every word has a vowel. The 5 vowels are: A, E, I, O, U. Each one makes a special sound!

A
"ah" or "ay"
🍎 Apple✈️ Cake
E
"eh" or "ee"
🥚 Egg🌳 Tree
I
"ih" or "eye"
🦋 Insect🧊 Ice
O
"oh" or "oo"
🐙 Octopus🦴 Bone
U
"uh" or "yoo"
☂️ Umbrella🦄 Unicorn
