Feelings Dictionary

😄
Joyful
Feeling very happy and full of excitement. You might feel joyful on your birthday!
😔
Gloomy
Feeling sad and down. You might feel gloomy when something doesn't go your way.
😤
Frustrated
Feeling annoyed when something is difficult or isn't working the way you want.
😰
Anxious
Feeling worried or nervous about something that might happen.
🤩
Excited
Feeling eager and happy about something that is going to happen soon.
😌
Calm
Feeling peaceful and relaxed. Not worried or upset about anything.
