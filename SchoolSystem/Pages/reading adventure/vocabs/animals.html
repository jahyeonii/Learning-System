Animal Dictionary

🦓
Zebra
A large African animal with black and white stripes. No two zebras have the same pattern!
🦒
Giraffe
The tallest animal on Earth. Its long neck helps it eat leaves from the tops of trees.
🐘
Elephant
The largest land animal. Elephants use their trunk to drink, grab food, and even hug!
🦜
Parrot
A colorful bird that can mimic — copy — the sounds and words that people say.
🦈
Shark
A powerful ocean fish with sharp teeth. Sharks are important predators in the sea.
🦔
Hedgehog
A small animal covered in sharp spines called quills. It rolls into a ball when scared!
